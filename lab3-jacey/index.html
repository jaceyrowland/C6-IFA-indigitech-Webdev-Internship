My Travel Bucket List

A list of dream destinations, attractions, best time to visit and estimated costs.
Destination Image Interesting Fact Why I Want to Go Must-See Attractions Best Time to Visit Estimated Travel Cost
Italy Italy has more UNESCO World Heritage Sites than any other country in the world — over 50! It’s also the birthplace of espresso, pizza, and some of the world’s most influential art and architecture. I want to travel around Italy to see the historical sites, history and the coffee. Colosseum, Venice Canals, Amalfi Coast April–June, Sept–Oct $3,200
Greece Greece is one of the sunniest countries on earth, with over 250 sunny days a year. It’s also home to one of the world’s oldest cities — Athens, inhabited for over 3,400 years. I want to see the cats, the beautiful islands and the historical sites. Santorini, Acropolis of Athens, Mykonos May–October $2,800
Salem, USA Salem is famous for the 1692 Witch Trials, but many historical sites there today are actually private homes still lived in — making it one of the most authentically preserved colonial towns in the U.S. Having learned about witchcraft and the history of witch trials, I want to visit. Witch House, Salem Witch Museum, Historic District, Boston's Anthropology Museum September–October $1,500
London, England London has over 170 museums, more than any other city in the world — including the British Museum, which has over 8 million objects. Also, Big Ben is not the tower — it’s the name of the bell inside the tower! I want to explore the culture, markets, and museums. Big Ben, Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, Historical Sites March–May, Sept–Nov $3,100
Paris, France The Eiffel Tower grows up to 6 inches taller in summer due to metal expansion. Paris is also known as the “City of Light” not just for its beauty — it was one of the first cities to adopt street lighting. Iconic art and romantic architecture. Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Seine River April–June, Sept–Oct $3,000
Estimated Total Cost $2,500
